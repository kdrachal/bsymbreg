The `bsymbreg` R package allows to perform the Bayesian symbolic regression as developed in the following papers: 

* Drachal, K.: Oil price forecasting with some genetic algorithm variable selection model. In: 6th AIEE Energy Symposium Conference Proceedings, Milano, December 2021, pp. 117-119. The Italian Association of Energy Economists (AIEE), Roma (2021) 

* Drachal, K.: Forecasting spot oil price with a genetic algorithm variable selection and Dynamic Model Averaging. In: Kolakovic, M., Horvatinovic, T., Turcic, I. (eds.) 6th Business & Entrepreneurial Economics (BEE) Conference – Book of Abstracts, Plitvicka Jezera, May 2021, p. 18. University of Zagreb, Zagreb (2021) 

* Drachal, K.: An example of a dynamic variable selection by a genetic algorithm in the large state-space model averaging scheme. In: Przegalinska, A., Kalkan, S., Aydin, E., Gokerik, M. (eds.) The 4th Economics, Business and Organization Research (EBOR) Conference – Abstract Book, Warszawa, May 2021, p. 93. EBOR Academy Publishing House, Konya (2021) 

* Drachal, K.: Some variable selection method with a genetic algorithm and model averaging: An application to forecasting oil price. In: Cermakova, K. (ed.) Book of Abstracts of the 15th Economics & Finance Conference, Praha, June 2021, p. 2. International Institute of Social and Economic Sciences, Praha (2021) 

The `bsymbreg` package requires the following packages: `graphics`, `MASS`, `Rcpp`, `RcppArmadillo`, `stats`, `utils`, `zoo`. They should be installed prior to the installation of the `bsymbreg` package. 

The `bsymbreg` package can be installed from GitHub:
``` r
devtools::install_github("kdrachal/bsymbreg") 
```
